Farming doesn't limit your resources to regular tools from wood and metal. This concept represents an app that provides all necessary data for getting heavy crops.


It shows a weather forecast, special notes about the user's fields, clear diagrams, and characteristics . Now instead of googling for ten minutes, you can open one app and prepare your crops.
